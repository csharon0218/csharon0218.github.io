About Me

I am an undergraduate student at Korea Aerospace University, majoring in AI and Autonomous Driving Systems Engineering with a double major in Software Engineering.

My academic interests lie in embodied AI, physical AI, autonomous driving, robotics, multimodal perception, and vision-language-action systems. I have gained hands-on experience through autonomous navigation, robot perception, LiDAR-based mapping, sensor fusion, object detection, imitation learning, and real-world robotic system deployment.

Through academic projects, research internships, competitions, and mentoring activities, I aim to develop intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and interact with complex real-world environments.
