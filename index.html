Minseo Choi

Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student at Korea University AI, interested in embodied AI, multimodal perception, and real-world robotics.

I completed my undergraduate studies at Korea Aerospace University, majoring in AI and Autonomous Driving Systems Engineering with a double major in Software Engineering.

Starting in September 2026, I will join the Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence at Korea University as an incoming Integrated M.S./Ph.D. student.

My research interests include embodied AI, multimodal representation learning, Vision-Language-Action models, imitation learning, reinforcement learning, human-robot interaction, and generalizable intelligent agents for real-world environments.

I have worked on robotics, autonomous driving, multimodal perception, ROS-based navigation, LiDAR mapping, sensor fusion, and learning-based control systems through research projects and internships.
