About me

Hi, I am Minseo Choi, an undergraduate student at Korea Aerospace University, majoring in AI and Autonomous Driving Systems Engineering with a double major in Software Engineering.

My research interests lie in Embodied AI, Physical AI, Vision-Language-Action models, imitation learning, reinforcement learning, multimodal perception, autonomous driving, and robotics.

I have gained hands-on experience through ROS-based robot navigation, LiDAR mapping, sensor fusion, autonomous driving platforms, YOLO-based perception systems, UAV vision systems, and real-world robotic system development.

My long-term goal is to develop intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and interact with complex real-world environments.
